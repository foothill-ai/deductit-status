DeductIt — Service Status

Updated: April 22, 2026 — 3:00 AM PDT

✅ Service restored

As of 2:58 AM PDT on April 22, 2026, DeductIt is fully back online. Sign-in, data sync, and all app features are operating normally on iOS, Android, and the web at deductit.io.

What happened

Starting in the early morning of April 21, our cloud hosting provider (Google Firebase) suspended the account that powers DeductIt, which took sign-in and data sync offline for roughly 24 hours. We have not yet received an adequate explanation from Google for the interruption, and we are continuing to pursue one.

Your data

Your data is safe. No donation records were lost during the interruption.

What we're focused on now

  • Post-mortem. We're conducting a full review of the incident — what happened, why it went undetected, and what we should have had in place to prevent it.
  • Robust fallback strategy. We're prioritizing a multi-provider backup architecture so that a single-provider disruption can never again take the service offline at this scale. This work is our top engineering priority until it ships.
  • Continued pressure on Google for a complete explanation of the root cause on their side.

Thank you for your patience through yesterday's disruption. If you ran into any issue that isn't resolved now that service is back, please email support@foothill.ai and we'll work with you directly.
